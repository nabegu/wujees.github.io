About

Wudil Journal of Earth and Environmental Sciences (WUJEES) is an online open access peer-reviewed journal which covers the field of Geography, Geology, environmental and earth sciences. It is a multidisciplinary academic publication that integrates all sciences for the study of the earth, environment and the solution of environmental problems. WUJEES publishes original articles, commentary, editorials, letters to the editor, review articles and case report describing original research in the fields of Earth and environmental sciences.


Wudil Journal of Earth and Environmental Sciences (WUJEES) is an Open Access journal, all the articles published will be accessible to all internet users throughout the world without any barrier of access under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution License.
